Managers Need Tools

🗓️Jun 29, 2005June 29, 2005🏷️ Management
📖 1 minute read

It seems that all professionals have their tools, but somehow managers were forgotten. No more! There is a great podcast to provide the tools that managers need to do their jobs better called Manager Tools.

The podcast is done by Mark Horstman and Mike Auzenne. They publish their podcast weekly and have a website with materials that every manager needs.

Check them out and listen weekly!
